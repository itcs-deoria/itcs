Courses Offered

Advanced Diploma in Information Technology (ADIT)

Our ADIT program is designed for students who want to master a broad range of IT skills. This course covers programming, software development, networking concepts, and database management in detail.

Students are encouraged to work on projects, participate in workshops, and collaborate in teams to simulate real-world IT challenges.

PC Fundamentals

This course is perfect for beginners. It covers the basics of computer operations, file management, and troubleshooting. Arun ensures the syllabus is completed within one month with a balance of theory and practice.

Daily tasks and hands-on assignments are given to students to help them gain confidence in working with computers.

Digital Marketing

Learn how to promote businesses online through social media, search engines, and email marketing. This course is designed for those looking to build a career in the fast-growing field of digital marketing.

Students will work on live campaigns and learn to analyze results for better performance.

CorelDRAW

Graphic designing is an essential skill in today’s market, and CorelDRAW is one of the best tools for creating stunning visuals. This course starts with the basics and progresses to advanced design techniques.

Students will create logos, brochures, and banners as part of their assignments to build a portfolio.

MS Access Software Development

Gain expertise in creating custom software solutions using MS Access. This course includes designing database structures, building forms, and automating workflows.

One of the highlights is the creation of an institute management system as a project.
